## Privacy policy

Colonize respects your privacy. The game runs primarily client-side,
and the scope of what reaches our servers is deliberately small.

### What we collect

- **Email address** — only if you opt in to the newsletter or, later,
  create a cloud-save account. Stored encrypted. Never sold, never
  shared with third parties for marketing.
- **Ad metrics** — the mobile builds (iOS and Android) display opt-in
  banner and rewarded ads through a third-party advertising SDK.
  Anonymous impression / click / conversion counts are transmitted to
  that SDK per its own privacy policy. No personally identifying
  information leaves your device through Colonize in the process.
- **Crash reports** — if you consent on first launch, anonymous
  technical crash traces are sent so we can fix bugs. No save-file
  contents, no account information.

### What we do not collect

- Real name, postal address, or payment data beyond what the app-store
  (Apple / Google) handles on their side.
- Device location, contacts, or anything outside the active game
  session.
- Save files — your campaign stays on your device unless you explicitly
  enable cloud save.

### Your rights

You may request deletion of any account email on file by writing to
[contact email]. Accounts with no cloud-save activity are purged
automatically after twelve months of inactivity.

### Changes to this policy

Material changes will be surfaced in-game on the next launch. The date
at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

## Terms of service

### Summary

Colonize is a single-player game (multiplayer planned for a later
release). Play it, enjoy it, and respect the other eventual players and
the infrastructure that keeps the service running.

### Permitted use

You may install and play Colonize on any device for which an official
build is available, for personal, non-commercial use. Your save files
are yours.

### Prohibited use

- Distributing modified copies of the game binaries.
- Automating account creation, ad-farming, or any activity designed to
  game future monetisation systems.
- Using exploits to obtain an unfair advantage in the planned
  multiplayer modes.
- Attempting to disrupt, overwhelm, or reverse-engineer the server
  infrastructure beyond what is necessary for interoperability and
  permitted by applicable law.

### Warranty

Colonize is provided "as is" without warranty of any kind, express or
implied. We will do our best to keep the cloud-save service available
once it launches, but we cannot guarantee uninterrupted availability.

### Liability

To the fullest extent permitted by applicable law, our liability to you
for any claim arising out of your use of Colonize is limited to the
amount you paid us for the game in the preceding twelve months (which
may be zero).
