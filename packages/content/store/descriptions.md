### Description (4000 char max)

```
A 2D retro 4X on a drowned world.

Earth is water now. The Collapse drained the old certainties and raised
new ones — pirate syndicates, salvaged starships that sail the sea, a
Concord tithe that keeps the peace at a price. You arrive in NW 2191,
the Early Liberty Era, with one battered hull and a crew of hopefuls.
Raise a colony, run the tithe, decide what to do when the deep answers
back.

FEATURES

• Turn-based 4X on a hand-drawn archipelago. Explore, expand, exploit,
  exterminate — the classic loop, retuned for a post-Collapse water
  world.
• Four playable factions at launch: Order of the Kraken, Ironclad
  Syndicate, Phantom Corsairs, Bloodborne Legion. Each with its own
  founding charter, sigil, and starting bonus.
• Colony management on salted reefs. Assign professions, queue
  buildings, balance hunger against ambition, keep the rum flowing.
• Ship combat under eldritch skies. Sloops, privateers, ships of the
  line — plus the occasional thing that should not be surfacing.
• The Rayon Concord tithe — a system-imposed schedule of tribute that
  every faction answers to, whether they flinch or fight.
• A Kraken Council that draws charters each session: unlockable edicts
  that reshape the rules of your run.
• Fragmentary canon. The world remembers more than it tells you; codex
  entries arrive piecemeal, tagged by certainty so you know what is
  established and what is rumour.
• Works offline. Single-player at launch. Cloud save and multiplayer
  planned for later releases.

WHO IT IS FOR

If you have ever restarted a Civilization run because the first city
site was wrong, or closed a Sid Meier's Colonization save to argue with
the founding fathers one more time, Colonize is aimed squarely at you.
We have kept the loop tight, the tiles chunky, and the UI keyboard-
friendly.

PRIVACY

Colonize runs primarily on your device. Email addresses reach our
servers only if you opt in to the newsletter or, later, a cloud-save
account. Mobile builds show opt-in banner and rewarded ads through a
third-party SDK; crash reports are opt-in. Full policy at
colonize.blacksail.dev/privacy.

CONTACT

Bug reports and feature requests: [support email].

Hic sunt dracones.
```

(~2100 chars.)
